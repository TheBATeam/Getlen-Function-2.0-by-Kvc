
 Calculates the Length of The Given String.

 Syntax: Call Getlen [String]
 Where

 String:	It is the String, Whose length to be calculated.

 The length of the string is Returned in to the Main fucntion through the
 Environmental Errorlevel variable.

 Try these lines in a Batch file: [E.g.]

 Call Getlen "Karan Veer Chouhan"
 Echo. %Errorlevel%
